How is this different from voicemail?

Voicemail waits and hopes someone leaves a message. 95% don't. CallIntakeHQ actually talks to your caller, asks the right questions, and delivers you structured job details — name, number, problem, urgency. It's the difference between losing a lead and rescuing a job.

Do I need to change my phone number?

No. You keep your existing business number. Just set up conditional call forwarding — when you can't answer, the call forwards to us. Takes 30 seconds on any phone. Your customers never see a different number.

Will callers know it's not a real person?

Most don't. The voice is natural, friendly, and uses your business name. It has a real conversation — not a robotic script. And honestly, your callers care about getting help, not who's picking up.

How fast do I get the job details?

Within 30 seconds of the call ending. You get an email with the caller's name, phone number, location, problem description, and urgency rating. Call them back fast and you'll land the job before they try someone else.

What if I want to cancel?
